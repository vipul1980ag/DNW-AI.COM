Who We Are

DNW-AI is the AI-first evolution of DNEXTWELT — a team of competent and accomplished SAP Consultants with deep specialisation in SAP Transportation Management (TM), Extended Warehouse Management (EWM), and Logistics Management (LGM).

Based in Mannheim, Germany, we serve enterprise clients across Europe and beyond, combining decades of SAP consulting heritage with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to deliver supply chain solutions that truly move the needle.

Our mission is simple: to help businesses move smarter — whether that means optimising a complex freight network, modernising a warehouse operation, or building an entirely new AI-powered logistics platform.
