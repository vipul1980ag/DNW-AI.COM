Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start an engagement?

For most projects, we can have a dedicated team mobilised within 2–3 weeks of contract signing. Managed services onboarding can begin within 5 business days.

Do you offer fixed-price project delivery?

Yes. For clearly scoped implementations we offer fixed-price contracts with defined milestones and deliverables. Time-and-materials arrangements are also available for more exploratory or agile engagements.

Can you work with our existing SAP landscape?

Absolutely. We perform a thorough landscape assessment before every engagement and are experienced working across SAP ECC, S/4HANA, TM, EWM, and surrounding systems.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes — we offer a complimentary 60-minute discovery call with one of our senior consultants. Use the form above or book directly via the "Book Online" button.
