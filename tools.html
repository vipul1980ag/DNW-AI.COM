Supply Chain Tips & Insights

Practical guidance from our consultants to help you stay ahead of the curve.

📊 Leverage Predictive Analytics

Use demand forecasting models to reduce safety stock by up to 30% while maintaining service levels. Start with your top 20% SKUs by revenue.

🔗 Integrate Your Data Sources

Siloed ERP, WMS, and TMS data is the biggest barrier to supply chain visibility. A unified data layer unlocks real-time decision-making.

🚀 Automate Repetitive Tasks

RPA and AI can handle routine order processing, invoice matching, and exception alerts — freeing your team for higher-value analysis.

🌱 Build Resilience Into Your Network

Dual-sourcing critical components and mapping sub-tier suppliers helps identify vulnerabilities before they become disruptions.

📱 Empower Mobile Workforces

Mobile-first warehouse and field operations apps reduce errors and accelerate task completion — SAP Fiori and custom apps are your friends.

♻️ Optimise Returns & Reverse Logistics

A streamlined reverse logistics process can recover 3–5% of revenue and significantly improve customer satisfaction scores.
